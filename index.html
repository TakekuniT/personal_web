About Me

Preview Resume

Hey guys, I'm Taki. Thank you for stopping by, whether you are my friends, family, recruiters, or even stalkers, I really appreciate it. If you want to learn more about my professional experiences and technical skills, please check out my resume above (I poured my heart and soul T^T). Otherwise, feel free to check out my projects and or my journey to where I am right now.

I first started my coding journey in grade school through Scratch , where I made bunch of simple games online. I really enjoyed it, and eventually ran a programming club at my high school, Coding with a Cause Club (CWAC) , with my friends (I designed their logo!). From there, I studied coding languages on my own, coded some projects, took AP CompSci, and eventually became a National Cyber Scholarship Competition Finalist in 2021. Pursuing a degree in computer science was a pretty easy choice for me.

I started my new journey at Stevens Institute of Technology in the fall of 2022. October of that year was also when I competed in my first hackathon , which was organized by SASE (I got 2 scholarships from them!). I also got my first job as a technical publication assistant at Stevens . After the end of my first year, I got a job at LIINES (I worked on their website!), after my second year I got a job as an AI trainer at Outlier and a software developer at VSC . In addition to my current jobs, I am particpating in the Headstarter Fellowship program as a SWE fellow. This portfolio was actually our first project!
